NOC Requirement

1. 2 Sets of Building Plans through Building Authority. 2. Building plans must contain all informations as prescribed by Bye-law No. 2.6 & 2.8 3. Filled in Questionnaire 4. Model of the Building 5. Check List Certified by the Architect Note : Delhi Building bye Laws for fire are mandatory.
