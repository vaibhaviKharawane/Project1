Other Information Related to Fire NOCs

Fire NOC Categories

The Government of Delhi provides Fire No Objection Certificates (NOCs) for various types of establishments. These include:

  • Residential Buildings
  • Commercial Establishments (e.g., malls, offices, shops)
  • Industrial Units
  • Educational Institutions
  • Hospitals and Medical Facilities

Fire NOC Process

The Fire NOC application process involves submitting the necessary documents, inspection of the site by the fire department, and fulfilling fire safety compliance measures. Key steps include:

  • Filing the online application through the official portal
  • Uploading relevant building plans and safety equipment details
  • Site inspection by the fire safety officer
  • Receiving the NOC after compliance with safety standards

Validity of Fire NOCs

Fire NOCs are generally valid for 1 to 5 years depending on the type of establishment. Renewals must be applied for before the expiration date by following the same process.
